Project: VANQUISHER
Role: Gameplay Programmer

I worked as a Gameplay Programmer using C# in Unity. I developed the third-person player controller including running, jumping and directional movement systems.

I also implemented the combat mechanics including input handling, attack logic and player combat interactions.

Additionally, I integrated character animations for movement and combat ensuring responsive gameplay experience.

A high-stakes third-person RPG set in the ancient Mayan kingdom where a warrior must face brutal trials and rise as the ultimate Vanquisher.

Every gameplay system was designed to support immersive combat and exploration.
