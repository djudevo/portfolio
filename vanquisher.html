VANQUISHER

Role: Gameplay Programmer

I worked as a Gameplay Programmer using C# in Unity. I developed the third-person player controller, including player movement mechanics such as running, jumping, and directional control.

I also designed and implemented the combat system, handling attack logic, input handling, and combat interactions. Additionally, I integrated and managed player animations to ensure smooth and responsive gameplay.

Key Contributions

  • Developed third-person player movement system
  • Implemented combat mechanics and attack system
  • Integrated character animations
  • Built the player controller using C# in Unity

Game Overview

A high-stakes third-person RPG set in the heart of the ancient Mayan Kingdom where a young warrior must face brutal trials, conquer the rites of passage, and rise as the ultimate VANQUISHER.

Controls

  • Movement: W A S D
  • Aim & Attack: Right-click to aim, Left-click to throw
  • Dodge: R
  • Start Fight: Walk to the wheel and press Spacebar
