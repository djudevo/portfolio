Vanquisher Game

Project: VANQUISHER Role: Gameplay Programmer I worked as a Gameplay Programmer using C# in Unity. I developed the third-person player controller, including player movement mechanics such as running, jumping, and directional control. I also designed and implemented the combat system, handling attack logic, input handling, and combat interactions. Additionally, I integrated and managed player animations for movement and combat to ensure smooth and responsive gameplay. Key Contributions Developed third-person player movement system Implemented combat mechanics and attack system Integrated character animations for movement and combat Built the player controller using C# in Unity A high-stakes third-person RPG set in the heart of the ancient Mayan Kingdom, where a young warrior must face the brutal trials of destiny, conquer the rites of passage, and rise as the ultimate VANQUISHER! Spin the Wheel of Fate to determine your foe—and the boon or bane that shapes your battle How to Play Movement: W A S D Aim & Attack: Right-click to aim, then Left-click to throw Dodge: R At Start-up: When you start walk towards the big wheel and press Spacebar to activate a fight with one of the three bosses
