Project: VANQUISHER

Role: Gameplay Programmer

I worked as a Gameplay Programmer using C# in Unity. I developed the third-person player controller including running, jumping and directional movement systems.

I also implemented combat mechanics including attack logic, player input handling and combat interactions.

Additionally, I integrated character animations ensuring smooth gameplay.

A third-person RPG set in the ancient Mayan kingdom where a warrior must face brutal trials and become the ultimate Vanquisher.
